FOREWORD
This book is one of a series of volumes, compiled by the
authority of the Army Council, the object of which is to preserve
the experience gained during the Second World War, 1939-1945,
in selected fields of military administration. The author has been
given access to official sources of information, and every endeavour
has been made to ensure the accuracy of the work as an historical
record. Any views expressed and conclusions drawn are those of
the author, and do not necessarily reflect those of the Army Council.
