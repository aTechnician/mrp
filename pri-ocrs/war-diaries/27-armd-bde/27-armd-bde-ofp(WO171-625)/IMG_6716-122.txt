Summary of Events and Information
Trade Test examination papers were marked and a discussion
held on the
results and questions.
The results were poor so decided to hold a further
test on O Nov. In the meantime lectures on Ord Procedure will be given.
A11 detnils of receipts and issues of Stripper Bars for Churchills given
to A.D.O.S.
Two Storemen sent to detachment with 256 Army Del Son to assist with re-
organization of Stores.
2 A.D.R, collected 3 Roney Shelters.
More Flying Bombs passed overhead.
Stores issued by C.O.D. Greenford to this Unit have been received from ADOS
2 Army Ips-addressed to ADOS/AR.
Selection and packing of stores surplus to our requirements and for issue to
17 Fa Trailer Sect continued.
Further two Storemen sent to 256 Army Del Son since there is little for them
to do at this H.Q. and they can be more usefully employed at Kitting Section.
