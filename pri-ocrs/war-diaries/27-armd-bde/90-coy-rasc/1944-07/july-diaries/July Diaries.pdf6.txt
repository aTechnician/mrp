Summary of Events and Information
1 x 15-Cwt Wireless Truck with 3 Operators attached to this Company HQ today
from Bde Sigs Sqn.
1 further 15-Cwt Wireless Truck temporarily attached to
Detachment at RANVILLE (104740) for inter-communication purposes.
3 small bombs dropped near Company Location at 0715 hrs, apparently from Allied
aircraft engaged in bombing S.E. of CAR.
At 2000 hrs instructions received from 27 Arma Bae to despatch 3 x 3-Ton Pet
and 3 x 3-Ton Derv to Detachment near RAWILLE ( 104740).
Lorries unable to
return owing to only Tastwards traffic being permitted across R.ORNE Bridges.
