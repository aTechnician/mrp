6. The preparation for a scheme
of demolitions will
include the selection of the proposed " belts," the allocation
of responsibility for the execution of the work, detailed
reconnaissance by the engineer and other services concerned
and the provision and collection of the necessary explosives,
labour and transport. It is essential, therefore, that the
need of demolitions should be foreseen
in ample time.
7. Further details of the organization of schemes of demoli-
tions are contained in Engineer Training. The technical
details of demolitions will be found in the manual of Military
Engineering, Vol. II, 1934, Chapter VII.
