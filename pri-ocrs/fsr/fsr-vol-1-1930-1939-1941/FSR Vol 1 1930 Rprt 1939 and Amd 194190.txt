31. The Q.M.G.'s branch at G.H.Q.
1. The Q.M.G.'s branch at G.H.Q. is responsible
for the initiation of the policy regarding all general
administrative matters which concern the duties of
the branch (see Sec. 10, 5), including the distribution,
construction and design of all depots holding animals
and stores throughout the theatre of operations. It is
responsible for the siting of such depots, consulting
the general staff on questions of tactical security. It
