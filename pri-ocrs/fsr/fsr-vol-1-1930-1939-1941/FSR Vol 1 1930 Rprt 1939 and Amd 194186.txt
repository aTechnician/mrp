Chap. V, Sec. 28.J
59
2. Six months after an individual has been reported
missing, and if he has not been satisfactorily accounted
for, the matter will be referred by the War Office to the
officer i/c 2nd echelon, who will cause inquiries to be
made and a report rendered. The procedure is set out
in Note (16), Appendix III.
