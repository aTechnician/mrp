[Chap. XIX, Sec. 182.
iv. Movable property belonging to the State which is
not directly susceptible of military use, such
as crown jewels, pictures, collections of works
of art and archives cannot be confiscated,
although papers in connection with the war
can be seized even if forming a part of archives.
Details of all property confiscated will be reported
immediately to G.H.Q. and to the central payment office
for requisitions, and such property will be handed over
to the representative of the service concerned, who will
report the details to the representative of his directorate
under whom he is serving.
