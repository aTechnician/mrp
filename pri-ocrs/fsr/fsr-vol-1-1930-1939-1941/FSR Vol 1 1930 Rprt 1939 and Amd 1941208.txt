103. G.H.Q. and corps transport
1. All transport units allotted to a corps-other than
divisional and corps troops groups of transport units
(Sec. 101, 1)- are under the direct administration of
the A.D.S.T. corps headquarters.
2. G.H.Q. transport consists of all transport units
not allotted to subordinate formations and is adminis-
tered by the head of the transport service at G.H.Q.
through the O.C. R.A.S.C., G.H.Q. troops.
