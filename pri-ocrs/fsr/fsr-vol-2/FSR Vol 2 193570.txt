3. When aircraft are employed to locate our own troops
in battle, they will be specially marked and will call for
flares or other means of identification from the troops by
firing a succession of white signal lights. The troops will
then indicate their position by lighting flares, by displaying
tin discs or by ground strips. The method to be used
must be previously laid down in orders.
