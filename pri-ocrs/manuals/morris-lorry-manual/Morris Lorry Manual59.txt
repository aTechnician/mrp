UNLOD
The small end of the special lever provided
in the tool kit should be inserted in the slot
which will be found near one end of the
spring flange.
LOP
A downward pressure of the lever
will
disengage a portion
of the flange from
the rim.
DUN
The free end should then be grasped
in the hand and the
flange drawn
away from the rim, which it will leave
quite early.
