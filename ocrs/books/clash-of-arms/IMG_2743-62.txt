units. Medium artillery comprised the 4.5-inch gun and 155mm M1 howitzer.

propelled gun, the 8-inch M2 howitzer, the 8-inch gun used for counterbattery
work, and the 240mm "Black Dragon" M1 howitzer. Stanton, Order of Battle,
