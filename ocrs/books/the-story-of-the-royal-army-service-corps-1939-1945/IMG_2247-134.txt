Rations reduced
From 1940 onwards the ration scale had to be constantly adjusted
according to the stocks available, but matters became really critical
in early 1942.
Between January and April it was only possible to
make two issues of frozen meat, and the ration of tinned meat was
drastically reduced.

be cut, and life was made no easier by the heavy and continuous air

which persisted until the advance of the Eighth Army to Benghazi

on December 7th, 1942.
Water Transport
