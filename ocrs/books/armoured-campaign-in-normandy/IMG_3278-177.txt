While the German attack had many shortcomings, a significant factor in is
defeat was the bravery of the US army forces in resisting the attack which, because
of the terrain, did not develop into the massed armour attack planned. Instead it
broke down into a number of small tank-infantry engagements, which were all
won decisively by the Americans.
