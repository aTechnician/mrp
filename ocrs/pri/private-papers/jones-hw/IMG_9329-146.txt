(2)
How To Speak.
R/T messages must be spoken in a way that will ensure maximum intelligibility at
the receiving end.
The following are the ma. i factors :
(1) RHYTHM.
Speak the message in short complete phrases that make sense, and not
word for word.
(2) SPEED.
Speak steadily at medium speed.
(3) VOLUME
Speak louder than in ordinary conversation, but do not shout.
(4) PITCH
Pitch your voice up and do not drop pitch on the last syllable of each
word and on the last word in each phrase as you would do in ordinary
conversation.
