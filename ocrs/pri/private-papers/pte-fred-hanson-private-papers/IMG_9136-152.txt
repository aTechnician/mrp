It was after the war ended that another Merchant Marine battle began. This time it was
for recognition and benefits from the U.S. govemment they heroically served. In 1987
they were given some recognition. During that year, a United States District judge ruled
that WWll merchant seamen were entitled to benefits granted in the G.I. Bill of Rights.
"A hollow victory," said one former Merchant Marine..
