RECOMMENDATION FOR 'MENTION IN DESPATCHES'
30.9.1944
"In the planning stages of this operation this NCO put in most valuable
work. He landed in the very early hours of D-Day and continued during
the first thirty days that this HQ was under shell fire to carry out his
duties always in an exemplary manner and was an example to all
subordinates.'
The citation was published in the London Gazette on 10 May 1945
