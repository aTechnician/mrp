Subject:- US Forces - Supply of Grease No' 3 2 & 3
RASC/3090/ST (FL
To:- 573 Signal Wiring Bn US Army
22 Group US Army, Pine Dene Ponteleand.
225 s/L AA Bn US Army, Drill Hall, Debdon Gardens.
From: - RASC Northumbrian District.
1. Will you please note that no further issues of Grease Nos 2 & 3 to USA Forces may
be made until the procedure laid down in para A of War Office letter BM. 3162/ST2
of 21 May 44 has been complied with by Units of US Forces.
2. Below copy of Para A for information.
"A. Units of the US Forces have been instructed to prepare requisitions covering their
requirements in triplicate, one copy will be passed to the British Supply depot,
one copy retained by the demanding unit and one despatched to the office of the
Chief Quartermaster H.Q, S.O.S., E.T. O.U.S. A., A.P. 0. 871 for editing and
approval. No grease will be issued until the latter copy has been returned to
the British supply depot and only the approved quantity will be issued to the
user unit."
OSFORTH
IN/LHM
F.W. Newall,
Cap to , Lto Colo
OC RASC Northumbrian District.
