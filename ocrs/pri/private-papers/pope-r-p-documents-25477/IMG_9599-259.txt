1. Regimental Canteens will be authorised only in cases whore NAFI are unable to

2. All Units wishing to conduct canteens regimentally for the Unit, or detachments

3. Formations will satisfy themselves before forwarding such applications to Command

Northern Command.

Formation to which the Unit belongs or of that which it may be joining.
