S/14332109 SGT RICHARD PERCIVALL POPE RASC
D-DAY 6 JUNE 1944
D-Day 6 June 1944
Group Landing Badge
worn by Sgt R P Pope RASC
FOUR DAYS BEFORE HIS 39th BIRTHDAY SGT POPE LANDED AT
HERMANVILLE SUR MER ON "SWORD" BEACH (QUEEN RED) IN
NORMANDY. HE LANDED ON D-DAY 6th JUNE 1944 AT 12 NOON
WHICH WAS 44 HOURS AFTER THE FIRST LANDINGS STARTED.
THE RED ANCHOR ON A BLUE BACKGROUND IS A BEACH GROUP
LANDING BADGE.
