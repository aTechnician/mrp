The coupons in this book are intended to cover your require-
ments to 31st May next.
4. You must not cut out coupons when buying goods. This must
be done by the shopkeeper, FOR WHOM IT IS ILLEGAL TO
ACCEPT LOOSE COUPONS except for mail orders. If you
order goods by post, you must, before you can get the goods,
cut out the required number of coupons, sign your name on the
back, and forward them to the trader.
5. If this book is lost, you will report the circumstances to your
Commanding Officer without delay.
6. This book is the property of H.M. Government and may only
be used for his/her own personal requirements by the person
to whom it was issued, who must be wearing uniform or produce
written evidence of identity when presenting the book to the
shopkeeper.
