The Westheer underwent a profound metamorphosis during the spring
of 1944. Instead of widening the gap between Allied and German capabili-
ties, as the OVERLORD planners had hoped postponement of an invasion
attempt until 1944 would, the gap closed. In seven months the Westheer
had gained a limited counterinvasion capability: it substantially strength-
ened its coastal defenses and the forces defending them, and it built up a
strong strategic reserve.
