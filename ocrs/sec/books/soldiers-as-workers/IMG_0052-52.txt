Even in the middle of campaigning, combed-out tailors finished new
issues of rough-and-ready uniforms. In the winter of 1812, this happened in
the goth Foot in Spain and hot-weather campaign clothing was issued to

War, soldiers regularly bought coarse brown cloth at local markets which the
regimental tailors made up into replacement issue trousers.34
