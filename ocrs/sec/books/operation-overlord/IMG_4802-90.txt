The Landings on the British-Canadian Beaches
The assault on the adjoining beaches went very
according to plan. Resistance was, by and large, no greater
than was anticipated. The preliminary bombardment was for
the most part fairly effective and because the sea, though
still rather stormy, had somewhat abated at the later H-hour,
fewer assault craft were casualties.
