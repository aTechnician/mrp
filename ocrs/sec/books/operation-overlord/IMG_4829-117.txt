DOCUMENTS
The chief source of the primary material used in this work was
the wartime files of Headquarters 12th Army Group. These files
were voluminous and covered the many aspects of the planning
and execution of the invasion of western Europe. Individual docu-
ments originated in a number of agencies-American, British, and
combined Allied-and, with some additions, proved adequate for
the purpose and scope of this book.
