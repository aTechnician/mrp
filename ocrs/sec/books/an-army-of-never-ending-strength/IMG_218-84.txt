On the last day of hostilities, the personnel levels within the division's units
were satisfactory. Losses had occurred during operations between 11 and 19
April, but reinforcements fill the gaps. Because of the importance of keeping
their units up to strength, a larger effort had been put into reinforcing the in-
fantry at the expense of other regiments. Although not at 100 percent of their
war establishment strengths, the noninfantry regiments were at adequate levels.
Their strengths were not affected drastically by combat operations in April.
