The armoured division variant of the RCA Anti-Tank Regiment as of January
1945 was a divisional antitank asset, as illustrated in Table A1.13. It could be
deployed where needed to bolster an infantry or armour brigade's antitank
capability. It contained four batteries in early 1945. Two batteries operated towed
17-pounder antitanks guns, and two batteries were self-propelled, operating the
Mio 17-pounder tank destroyers. It also operated Universal Carriers, 20 mm
antiaircraft guns, and 2-inch mortars. "B" vehicles consisted of 28 cars and 103
trucks. Of the latter, 26 were artillery tractors. This vehicle total was supple-
mented by twenty-six trailers and twenty motorcycles. The RCEME LAD type
"D" strength was one officer and forty-two other ranks.
