The January 1945 Canadian towed RCA Field Regiment infantry division
variant was a divisional artillery asset, as illustrated in Table At.7. It consisted
of three batteries and a regimental HQ. Its batteries were equipped with the
towed 25-pounder howitzers rather than self-propelled variants. There were
three field regiments within each infantry division for a total of nine infantry
division variants in northwest Europe by spring 1945. This regiment contained
a different war establishment than the self-propelled and towed field regiments
